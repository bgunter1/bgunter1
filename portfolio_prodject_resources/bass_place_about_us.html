Hello my name is Brandon the founder of Bass Place. A few years ago I decided to pick up bass playing. While trying to learn and grow as a player I realised its not to easy to make connections and get to know other bass players. I wanted to come up with a website that can help build a community for bass players to interact with. A place you can call for advice and develope your skills. Also a place where you can connect and make friends with fellow bass players.
